About

Profile

Perth-based filmmaker

I'm Connor, a Perth-based filmmaker. Films have been an integral part of my life for as long as I can remember, and it is my greatest joy to document people's stories through my lens. I am captivated by both the quiet and unspoken beauty of everyday life, as well as the grandeur of our most unforgettable moments.

After completing my Bachelor of Film at SAE University and spending several years working across marketing and freelance projects, I have developed a strong eye in photography and videography, as well as many technical skills including cinematography, editing, sound design, colour grading, content creation and more.

What I shoot

I have extensive experience shooting cars, construction and sports, and also have experience producing work for music, corporate events and weddings. This diverse range of projects has allowed me to develop a flexible and adaptable approach to filmmaking. I love to challenge myself and reach outside my comfort zone, constantly finding new and unique ways to flex my creative muscles.
